Different Types of Cat Toys


Interactive Cat Feather Wand

This is a classic cat toy that features a wand with feather. This cat toy is perfect for your cat to get more exercise, improve their agility and immunity, reduce obesity. It is a good way to interact and bonds with your cat.

Interactive Roller Track

Complete toy with a circle base, a spinning ball and a mouse motion to exercise cat's natural instinct with fun. Perfect to keep your cat healthy and active which helps satisfies your cat's natural instinct.

Catnip Soft Pouches

These toys are made from plush fabric, stuffed with catnip, soft and comfortable, non-toxic and safe for cats to bite and chew. Attracts your cat to kick, play, swoop, bite and chase, lets it more active and helps to regulate emotions and release stress.


Play Treat Puzzle

This cat toys combine cat puzzle toys, cat treat toys, and cat ball toys. Excellent various functions excite kittens' desire to play and provide them more entertainment. This pet cat food toy can also be used as a cat puzzle feeder, which can slow down your cats eating speed, and keep your cats away from overeating and obesity.

Cat Tree Tower

The cat tree keep feline fun on the rise, with plenty of jumping platforms and napping area. These platforms can be used for both rest and exercise. They could lie on it to have a relax and enjoy the scenery in the distance.

Fast Cat Exercise Wheel

If your cat has some serious energy, this could be just what you need to keep her well-exercised and happy. Essentially an oversized hamster wheel, this toy lets your cat walk or run as she pleases. It's especially helpful if you live in a small apartment where your kitty might not have room to sprint, but make sure you have the space for the setup as it is a larger item.


Electric Butterfly Rotating Cat Teaser

This toy flies around unpredictably, driving your cat wild and encouraging active play. With just a touch of the button and a gentle push, the battery-operated butterfly starts to rotate. The natural movement of the butterfly will stimulate your cat's hunting instincts and encourage it to give chase.

Whack a Mole Cat Toy

It's a combination toy of whack a mole game, cat scratching board and teaser stick. This fun cat toy will quickly warm your cats up and helps to train your cat's intelligent. All this will help to consume extra energy, relieve their boredom, lose weight and stimulate curiosity, which also relieve scratches to the furniture.

Five-Way Tunnel

This five-way tunnel is the perfect outlet for cat zoomies. With each opening measuring 11 inches (Large enough for even the chunkiest chonks.) Most tunnel usually come with a built-in jingly ball and catnip toy to encourage playtime and entice stalking.


Catit Design Senses Super Roller Circuit

Catit Design Senses Super Roller Circuit takes fun and action to breathtakingly new heights. This large 8-piece circuit has alternating high and low sections with an enticing motion-activated illuminated ball the zips around inside, enticing cats to chase and swipe at it.

Tower of Tracks

Three tracks, three balls, three times the fun. This tower toy lets your cat bat and chase the colorful balls until she tuckers herself out for a nap. The non-slip base keeps the toy in place, and the balls always stay on the track so there's no risk of losing them under a piece of furniture.

Electric Dancing Fish Cat Catnip Toy

This floppy fish cat toy can move and wiggle itself automatically when it is being touch. With a detachable electric and you can charge it with a USB cable. This cat fish toy is stuffed with catnip which can excite your cat and relieve your cat's stress.


Swimming Led Light Fish Toy

The swimming fish toy made of food highquality material, safe, non-toxic, harmless to pets and kids. The swimming fish toy with automated fins that automatically begin moving around as soon as they're placed into the water without additional control.

Cat Balls Assortments

Each ball has a different texture for your cat to explore, with each ball emitting a different sound to entice your cat and spark those natural chase instincts. Your cat will love whacking, chasing and pouncing on these balls, helping to keep your cat healthy and active.

Cat Play Mat

This cat play rug makes almost every cat play. Whether it's your favorite toy mouse, a cat fishing rod playing together, treats or a cat buddy, whatever hides under the play mat is hunted. Our domestic tigers are still hunters inside and hiding the prey in the small caves is irresistible for almost every cat. The play mat is for all cats and promotes dexterity, movement, the inner hunting instinct and above all fun and belongs in my opinion in every cat household.
